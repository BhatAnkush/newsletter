IPRS CAMP 2022

Interpersonal Relationship Skills Training

Camp for the scholars was held after a gap of 2 long years. Interpersonal Relationship Skills (IPRS) is a new module created by our experts in the field. This programme aims to help scholars to understand the concepts, especially skills and attributes associated with emotional intelligence or being able to understand and manage one's own and other's emotions.

IPRS was a four-day residential camp held during KSHAMATA season from 14th April 2022 to 17th April 2022. 101 Scholars attended the camp.
